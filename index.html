About Me

I've always felt a pull towards the online world, and recently, life has nudged me to embrace it fully. Over the past month, I've immersed myself in learning Git, the foundations of web development, Python, Linux, and a bit of networking, including IP addressing, subnetting, and TCP/IP. I believe that traditional jobs might not be my calling; instead, I see my future in building online businesses from the ground up. This journey not only allows me to explore my passion for web development but also equips me with the skills needed for a potential career as a DevOps engineer. My goal is to create websites that are functional, aesthetically pleasing, and effective in meeting user needs. I'm excited about the endless possibilities that lie ahead as I continue to grow in this ever-evolving field.
